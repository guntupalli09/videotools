## Description (500 words max)

**VideoText** is a fast, privacy-first video processing platform built for creators, podcasters, and agencies.

**What it does:**
Upload your video and get back transcripts, subtitles, or translations in seconds — not minutes. We support 50+ languages and handle the full workflow in one place.

**7 tools in one:**
- Video → Transcript (with speakers, summary, chapters, keywords)
- Video → Subtitles (SRT/VTT)
- Translate Subtitles (50+ languages)
- Fix Subtitles (auto-correct timing, remove fillers, fix line breaks)
- Burn Subtitles into video (hardcoded captions)
- Compress Video (light / medium / heavy)
- Batch Process (up to 100 videos at once, ZIP export)

**The thing that makes us different:**
We don't store your files. Your video is processed and immediately deleted. No exceptions. This matters if you work with client footage, confidential recordings, or anything you'd rather not leave on someone else's server.

**Who it's for:**
- YouTube creators who need transcripts for SEO and subtitles for retention
- Podcasters turning recordings into searchable show notes
- Agencies processing client videos with batch export
- Remote teams transcribing Zoom/Teams calls with speaker labels and action items

**Free tier — no credit card:**
3 free imports to try every tool. No account required to start.

**Numbers:**
- 2M+ minutes transcribed
- 50+ languages
- 98.5% accuracy
- 2,000+ creators using it

We built this because existing tools were either too slow, stored your files indefinitely, or required stitching together 3–4 different products. VideoText replaces all of them.
