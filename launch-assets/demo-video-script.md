## RECORDING TIPS

- Record at 1920×1080, then export at 1280×720 for PH
- Use a real video file you have — ideally a 3–5 minute clip so processing is visibly fast
- Don't show the full processing time — cut to "transcript appears" to keep pace
- Add subtle background music (Uppbeat.io has free-for-creators tracks)
- Voiceover: record separately in Audacity or GarageBand, then sync
