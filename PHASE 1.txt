   - Check: subtitle[i].endTime > subtitle[i+1].startTime
   - Fix: Set subtitle[i].endTime = subtitle[i+1].startTime - 0.1

   - Check: subtitle.text.length > 42
   - Fix: Split into two lines at nearest space before char 21

   - Check: (endTime - startTime) < 1.5 seconds AND text.length > 20
   - Fix: Extend endTime to ensure minimum 1.5s display

   - Check: subtitle[i+1].startTime - subtitle[i].endTime > 5
   - Flag as potential issue (don't auto-fix, just report)
